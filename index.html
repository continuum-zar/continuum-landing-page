Frictionless execution & recording

Stop fighting your tools to track work. Team members simply click to start recording time directly on a task — no separate timesheet app and no manual reconciliation. With seamless invoicing, tracked time flows straight into invoices, so there is no more chasing down timesheets or reconciling data across disconnected systems.

Your clients see progress, not your process

Continuum uses role-based access control with three core roles and no configuration overhead. Admins get full control over projects, billing, team, and audit logs. Members work in assigned sprints, log time, and see only what is relevant to their work. Continuum AI is read-only by default — handling planning, task drafting, and status updates in the background without changing what is on the sprint board. Clients receive a curated view of milestones and delivery status, never the raw sprint board.

Easy migration with no forced cutover

Import your existing board from Jira, Trello, Asana, or a generic CSV — your structure comes with it. Columns, labels, and assignees transfer automatically. There is no forced cutover: your team can work in both tools until the switch is done, and your first sprint can be live in under an hour.

Built for teams that can't afford to get security wrong

POPIA compliance isn't a checkbox added after the fact — it's built into how Continuum stores, logs, and manages your data. Continuum aligns with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), applying encryption in transit, role-based access controls, and POPIA-aligned retention. Production data runs on managed cloud infrastructure (PostgreSQL and application services deployed via Railway), and sensitive credentials are stored encrypted or hashed, never in plaintext.

Frequently asked questions

Is Continuum POPIA compliant?

Yes. Continuum is built with POPIA in mind. Our Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information, your rights under POPIA, and how to reach our Information Officer at info@joincontinuum.co.za. We apply encryption in transit, role-based access controls, and POPIA-aligned retention.

Who can see my team's sprint board?

Only people you explicitly invite to a project. Sprint boards and Kanban views require project membership and the right permissions. Clients and view-only members get a curated experience of milestones and progress, not internal diagnostics like raw sprint boards or team hours.

Which project management tools can I import from?

Jira (CSV export), Trello (JSON export), Asana (CSV export), and generic CSV files. The import wizard auto-detects the source format, or you can pick it manually for heavily customised exports.

How quickly can my team be running their first sprint?

Most teams can have a first sprint live in under an hour — create a project, invite members with the right roles, and build or import a board.
